Hi! I am Riccardo Ennio

Animator and Visual Storyteller.

As a visual storyteller, I invest time, patience, and craft, seeking a deep connection between my art and its audience. In my works, I explore the interplay between the materiality of traditional animation and its digital transcription. My heart beats for ink and paper, always my starting point in a process which culminates in collaboration with the digital. I have a keen interest in using visual storytelling as an accessible means to address weighty subjects with a gentle touch. My commitment lies in preserving the human element in storytelling, providing an antidote to the alienating effects of automation in our increasingly technologised society.
