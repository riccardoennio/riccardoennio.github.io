Who Moved My Desk?

Animation exercise featuring a dialogue between a 2D character and a 3D character.

The audio file is taken from an episode of the series "The Office."
The 2D character featured is Kent from "The Iron Giant" (1999), and the animation related to him was created using TVPaint.
The 3D character is Mars from the M-Rig Bundle, with the associated animation made in Maya.
