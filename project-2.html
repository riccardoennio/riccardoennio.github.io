Gilded Cage

An elderly woman finds herself trapped in a virtual memory loop after enrolling in a program designed to let her relive her best memories. Desperate to escape, she resorts to extreme measures, waging a battle within her own mind to break free from the simulation.

The animatic was created using TVPaint, while the visual development was done in Photoshop.
